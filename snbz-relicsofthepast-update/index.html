RELICS OF THE PAST UPDATE VIDEO

It's Here

Welcome to the new story of Below Zero

Voiced Dialogue

We couldn’t be more excited to unveil a final, fully-voiced story. It’s been quite the journey, but we are so proud of the final product. We hope you enjoy it as much as we do. Thank you to everyone who participated in this process, from our actors, to our writers, to you, our fans.

Talking Portraits

Character portraits now appear in the upper left to indicate speaker during voiced dialogue. There are a wide number of characters in our story, all of whom are clearly depicted in sync with their spoken lines.

PDAs, Databanks, & Encyclopedias

Get your scanner ready, this update adds hundreds of text-entries for your reading pleasure. From logs left by named and unnamed characters to encyclopedia entries, there is something for everyone.

Robin's New Look

Crash-landing on a new planet is no small task. Robin’s new look includes a new model and a (fancy) new dive suit. She is more ready than ever for whatever this watery planet has in store for her.

Time to Dive Back in

Beacons & Inventory

On death, beacons now drop alongside the rest of your inventory. Because of this, make sure to keep a beacon on hand so your precious things may be recovered in the event of an unfortunate accident.

Posters

Keep your eyes peeled for new posters dotted around the world. Each poster tells a story and some of them are from our very own community. Feel free to intact with and inspect pictures in frames.

Wander too deep in the arctic region of 4546B and you’re bound to make friends with the Void Chelicerate who hunt at extreme depths. Well, they will enjoy your company more than you will enjoy theirs. Best to steer clear of this dark and dangerous part of the world.

Precursor Caches

Hidden around the planet, finished Precursor caches flicker to life. These glowing rooms contain mysterious flora, curious technology, and irresistible mystery.

Cleanup and polish work continues around 4546B. At every turn, something surprising and amazing awaits. Areas that once felt familiar will feel completely refreshed.

PDA Pause

From the Accessibility Menu, it’s now possible to enable a PDA Pause. By default, the game will continue as you look at your PDA. However, enabling PDA Pause will halt gameplay instead.
